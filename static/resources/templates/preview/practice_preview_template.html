Once you have completed it, you will be able to take the main task, which is called {{ jg['main_task_name'] }}. You will see multiple groups of images, one after the other. Your job will change depending on the section; in some sections, you will choose the most {% filter upper %}{{ jg['attribute'] }}{% endfilter %} image out of that group, in others you will chose the least {% filter upper %}{{ jg['attribute'] }}{% endfilter %}. However, in some HITs, you may be asked to only choose the most {% filter upper %}{{ jg['attribute'] }}{% endfilter %}, or only choose the least.
